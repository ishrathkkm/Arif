About Us

The journey of a simple venture which now have become the choice of generations. Story of Ashraf Saree Center birth started in 1970's when Ungungal Abdull Kutty Haji started a small cloth centre at Madanapalle,Andhra Pradesh. Though over the years it is grown, developed and spread, the light of service based of love he lit still kept unaffected. This is the sole emotion which binds Ashraf Saree Center and its countless customers together. We realize this not our greatness but the support and love coming from all corners.

Abdulla Kutty Haji(Managing Director)

The goodwill of business is not simply the turnover the company makes year by year. I strongly believe goodwill is an attitude and the service an establishment gives out to the society… The message of togetherness that the mentors spread among the customers. The motto of our business is not huge profit. But minimal profit assuring quality and variety. When these aspects stand as the criteria, Faith takes birth and that is the most important thing which lead a venture forward… And I assert these are the things behind our glorious tradition and also taking us towards fulfillment. The effort is not an individual’s ideology.. but a teamwork.. Everybody as a family.. The management, Staff and the customers work together to make Ashraf Saree Center which is built upon values and goodness , Stand victorious ever.
